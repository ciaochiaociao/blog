## Tools

- zoterobib ([https://zbib.org/](https://zbib.org/))

  - inputs: url, doi (no bibtex)
  - ~10k formats
  - **ACM**, ACL, ...

- BibTex Online Converter ([https://bibtex.online/](https://bibtex.online/))

  - inputs: bibtex
  - only 4 formats but no ACM (**Chicago**)
